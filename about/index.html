Close Encoders

Hi, my name is Josh.

I write code and tinker with computers mostly for fun, then occasionally play games when I'm bored.

While in a professional setting, I am pretty organized, in contrast, my personal projects and learning tend to be very arcane.

Imagine a raccoon finding something shiny on a whim, then immediately forgetting about it once they are bored.

So I made this site as a hub for recording adventures and hopefully documenting the chaos.

My why is simple: as long as it does not hurt others, I believe that with enough effort, anyone can build or do anything they want.

You may have to become obsessed, sell your soul, and make trad offs in the process... but truly anything.

If you become skilled enough, no one can tell you otherwise or stop you from pursuing your goal.
